Hello! I'm Dana.

I am a UX Designer based in Vancouver, Canada with a background in writing, user research, and visual design.

I am currently completing a Bachelors of Science at the School of Arts and Technology at the University of Simon Fraser. I began loving design in high school when my mom gave me my first, non-paid, job designing posters for events that her daycare hosted. I remember thoroughly enjoying the process of designing those posters and getting feedback on where things could be improved. Although looking back, those posters needed a lot of improving :) I also managed the daycares social medias creating a coherent layout and color scheme that is still used today by the new media design team!

I worked in a variety of different fields after that such as a marketing associate and customer service representative; more can be found on my resume! Overall, I would say that I am an extremely flexible and open minded person that can easily adapt in any workplace. I am currently looking for employment opportunities in the design field with the hopes of increasing my skills and learning the ins and outs of the design force.
